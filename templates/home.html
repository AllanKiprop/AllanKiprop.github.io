What are Misoji Stickers?

Learning English can surely be a dreaded feat, especially to pupils with unfavorable cradle foundations. Trying to solve this issue in later stages can prove to be difficult. However, with fun, engaging MISOJI stickers, positive attitudes are bound to be built, creating motivation, consistency and better learning outcomes.
